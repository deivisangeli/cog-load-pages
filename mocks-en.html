Cog-Load App — mock screens · EN

Portuguese version: mocks-pt.html · Cog Load home

3-agent architecture. Marina is Carolina's POC (woman-coded, pink chrome). Tiago is José's POC (man-coded, blue chrome). Pivot is the chief-of-staff: genderless, sees both sides, surfaces only at formal moments (proposals, agreements, escalations). Neither POC ever reads the other partner's chat.

private = visible only to that partner · shared = both partners see it · M# = treatment mechanism activated.
Note: setting is Brazilian middle-class; mockups in English are for non-PT readers. Names stay as in the production scenario.
