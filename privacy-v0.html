Cog Load — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-03 · Status: development pilot.

Cog Load is a research-and-utility pilot that helps couples manage household mental load through a WhatsApp-based assistant. This page describes what data we collect, why, and how to control it.

Who runs this

A small multi-author research team. Build lead: Deivis Angeli (angeli@globtalent.org). The project is currently in private development; only people who have been explicitly invited and who have given consent in WhatsApp can interact with it.

What we collect

  • Your WhatsApp phone number and the name you tell us to call you.
  • Messages you send to your assigned assistant (text and voice transcripts).
  • Tasks, reminders, schedule blocks, and stable preferences you ask us to remember.
  • Basic household context you choose to share (e.g., number of children, age band). We do not store children's names.

Why we collect it

To deliver the assistant features you signed up for: reminders, task tracking, schedule coordination, and (with both partners' consent) negotiated reallocations of household work. The legal basis is your explicit consent, given in WhatsApp before any data is stored.

Where it lives

Servers in Brazil (São Paulo region). Voice messages are transcribed via OpenAI's API under a Zero-Data-Retention agreement, meaning OpenAI does not store the audio or text. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

How long we keep it

As long as your account is active. If you ask us to delete your account, all your personal data is removed within 24 hours. If you separately consent to donate anonymized data to research, that anonymized record may be retained per the research protocol; you can revoke that anytime.

Your rights

Under Brazil's LGPD (and equivalent laws elsewhere) you can:

  • Ask what data we have about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Export your data in JSON or CSV within 24 hours of request.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • File a complaint with the Brazilian data protection authority (ANPD, gov.br/anpd).

Privacy between partners

The assistant runs two separate, private conversations — one per partner. Your messages are never visible to your partner. The mediation features only act on formal proposals you explicitly approve before they are forwarded.

How to delete your data or contact us

Send "apagar meus dados" / "delete my data" to your assistant on WhatsApp, or email angeli@globtalent.org. We respond within 72 hours.

This policy will be revised before the project leaves private development. The controlling legal entity for the production pilot is to be confirmed jointly by the project's coauthors before any open enrollment.
