Cog Load — Terms of Service

Last updated: 2026-05-03 · Status: development pilot.

Cog Load is a research-and-utility pilot. By using the WhatsApp assistant, you agree to these terms.

What it is

A messaging-based assistant that helps you organize household tasks, schedules, and (with your partner's consent) coordinate task allocation. It is not a substitute for medical, legal, financial, or therapy advice. It is not designed for crisis intervention.

Use is invitation-only

The pilot is currently restricted to people who have been explicitly invited and have provided consent. There is no open signup.

What we will and won't do

We will: respond to your messages, store the data you ask us to remember, send reminders, and (only with both partners' explicit approval) facilitate negotiated task reallocations.

We will not: forward raw chat content from one partner to the other, share your data with third parties for marketing, or use your data outside the purposes you consented to.

Crisis

If you mention thoughts of harming yourself or someone else, the assistant may suggest contacting the CVV (188 in Brazil, 24h, free) or local emergency services. This pilot is not equipped to handle crisis situations directly.

Liability

This is a development pilot offered "as is." We do our best, but reminders and suggestions may occasionally be late, wrong, or missed entirely. Critical medical, legal, or safety decisions should not depend solely on this service.

Termination

You can stop using the service at any time by asking the assistant to delete your data, or by emailing angeli@globtalent.org. We may also pause or end your access if we suspect misuse.

Governing law

Brazilian law (LGPD and Marco Civil da Internet) governs use of this service for participants in Brazil. Disputes are subject to the courts of Niterói/RJ unless local consumer-protection law assigns a different jurisdiction.
